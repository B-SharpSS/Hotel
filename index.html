WELCOME TO OUR PALACE

The High Castle

The High Castle Hotel, is but the pinnacle of luxury. Not only have we imported the Danyian Fortress from Mongolia to Sweden, but also transformed it into a one of a kind, state of the art, luxurious hotel. Here, you will be able to live in the same luxuries that Genghis Khan and his 25 concubines once enjoyed. Our goal is to not only provide you with the outmost of comfort, but also to please your soul, and that is why the beauty of our establishment is the top priority.
